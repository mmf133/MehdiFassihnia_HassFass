Our Story About Our Avocados

In 2016, two brothers, Mehdi and Amir, took a leap of faith and ventured into a world they had no experience in: producing avocados. The idea of planting Hass avocados came from a Costa Rican goventment decision to ban avocados imports from Mexico back in May 2015. The rich volcanic soils of the Poas Volcano in Costa Rica found a perfect home to our avocados, where we have continued to grow both in knowledge and size.

As a family owned business, we guarantee that you will taste the love and dedication we have put into growing your avocados.
